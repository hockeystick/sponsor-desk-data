# Onpoint Cooperative declares $58 million member dividend for 2025

*Portland, Oregon — December 9, 2025.* The Onpoint Cooperative board
of directors voted on December 8, 2025, to declare a member dividend
of approximately $58 million for the 2025 fiscal year, returned to
members in proportion to qualifying share balances and loan activity
during the year. The dividend will appear in member accounts in
January 2026.

The 2025 dividend reflects the cooperative's net income of
approximately $112 million for the year, of which the board
allocated $58 million to the member dividend, $26 million to the
Branch Reinvestment Initiative, $9 million to community-development
grant programming, and the remainder to retained earnings consistent
with the credit union's capital plan.

"This is a meaningful return to members, and it is also a recurring
annual rebate that members should be able to count on regardless of
any single year's headline number," said Linnea Tørgersen, president
and chief executive officer of Onpoint Cooperative. "Two member
groups asked the board this year to allocate differently — one
toward larger per-member rebates, one toward larger community-
development spending. Both critiques are reasonable. The 2026 board
will publish a written allocation rationale alongside next year's
dividend declaration."

The 2025 dividend amount is consistent with the credit union's
average annual member-dividend allocation of approximately 50% of
net income over the prior five years. The Branch Reinvestment
Initiative allocation funds the second year of the five-branch
refresh program covering Beaverton, Hillsboro, Hood River, Bend, and
Astoria.

Onpoint chief lending officer Andrew Brightwater noted that 2025
loan activity contributing to the dividend was driven primarily by
auto lending, including continued growth in the EV Drive Forward
Auto Loan product, and by mortgage refinancing in the second half
of the year as benchmark rates moved.

The 2025 financial statements will be published in full in the
credit union's annual report in March 2026.
